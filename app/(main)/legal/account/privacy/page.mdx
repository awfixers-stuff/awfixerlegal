# Privacy Policy for Account

This page describes how **Account** specifically handles privacy and data protection. For general provisions including contact information, your rights, and how to exercise them, see our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).

## Authentication Data

We collect your email address and password when you create an account. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms before storage. We never store passwords in plain text.

If you use single sign-on through a provider like Google or Microsoft, we receive your name, email address, and profile information from that provider. Passwords from the identity provider are never received or stored by us.

You can enable two-factor authentication for additional security. If you do, we store your phone number or authentication device details as needed to verify your identity.

## Billing and Payments

We use Stripe to process payments. When you add a payment method, Stripe collects your card details directly. We receive only the last four digits of your card, the expiration date, and your billing address. We do not store full card numbers.

Billing history, invoices, and subscription status are stored in your account for as long as you maintain an active subscription or as required by law.

## API Keys

You can generate API keys to access AWFixer services programmatically. API keys are hashed before storage. We display the full key only once, at creation. If you lose a key, you must generate a new one.

API key usage is logged. You can view recent usage from your account settings. Revoking a key immediately stops all access associated with it.

## Admin Data

If you are a workspace owner or administrator, we collect additional data to manage your team. This includes member lists, role assignments, and audit logs of administrative actions.

Admin actions, such as inviting members, changing permissions, or deleting content, are logged and retained for security and compliance purposes. These logs are visible to other administrators in the same workspace.

## User Profiles

Your profile includes your name, email, profile photo, and any optional information you choose to add. Other workspace members can see your name and photo. Your email visibility depends on your workspace settings.

You can edit or remove your profile information at any time from your account settings. Removing your profile photo reverts it to a default avatar.
