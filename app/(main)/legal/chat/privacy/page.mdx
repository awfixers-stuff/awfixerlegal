# Privacy Policy for Chat

This page describes how **Chat** specifically handles privacy and data protection. For general provisions including contact information, your rights, and how to exercise them, see our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).

## AI and LLM Processing

Chat uses large language models to generate responses to your prompts. When you send a message, we forward your input to our AI providers. These providers process the text and return a generated response.

Your prompts and the resulting outputs are stored so you can review your conversation history. This data is tied to your account and workspace. Other members of your workspace cannot see your private chats unless you explicitly share them.

## Conversation Data

We store the full content of your conversations, including messages, attachments, and metadata such as timestamps. This lets you pick up where you left off and search past discussions.

Workspace administrators can see conversation metadata, such as usage statistics and the number of messages sent. They cannot read the content of private conversations unless you share them or export them.

## Embeddings and Search

To power semantic search inside Chat, we create vector embeddings of your messages. These embeddings are mathematical representations of text that let us find related content quickly. The embedding data is stored in our systems and is not sent to third parties.

## Third-Party AI Providers

We use multiple AI providers to deliver Chat features. Each provider has its own data handling practices.

For enterprise customers, we configure our AI providers to apply zero data retention. This means your prompts and outputs are not used to train or improve the provider's models. Standard accounts may use providers with standard retention policies.

We list our current AI providers in our [Subprocessors](/legal/subprocessors) page.

## Prompt Data Handling

We do not use your prompts or outputs to train our own models without your explicit consent. Your conversation data is used only to deliver the Chat service to you.

In rare cases, we may review anonymized or aggregated prompt patterns to detect abuse, spam, or policy violations. This review is automated where possible and human-reviewed only when necessary for safety.
