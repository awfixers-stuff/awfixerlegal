# Privacy Policy

At AWFixer, we respect your need for privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices. It applies to all AWFixer services, including **Chat**, **Grip**, **Workspace**, and **Account**.

## Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and data generated through your use of our services.

**Account information.** When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and password. You can add a profile photo later. We use this to identify you and manage your account.

**Usage data.** We collect data about how you interact with our products. This includes log data, device information, browser type, IP address, and timestamps. We use this to keep services running and spot problems early.

**Customer content.** You store files, messages, configurations, and other content in our services. We process this data to provide requested features. We do not use your content to train AI models unless you opt in.

**Payment data.** We use Stripe to process payments. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers. Stripe handles that under its own privacy standards.

**AI inputs and outputs.** If you use AI features, we send your prompts and receive generated responses through third-party AI providers. We describe how this works in our product-specific privacy pages.

## How We Use Information

We use your information for straightforward purposes.

Our services depend on your data. Your account information lets you log in. Your content enables collaboration. Usage data helps us fix bugs and improve performance.

Communication is part of our service. Transactional emails include password resets, billing receipts, and security alerts. Product updates and tips may also reach your inbox. You can opt out of marketing emails anytime.

Product improvement drives our analysis. Aggregated usage patterns show us what features matter. We never sell this analysis to outside parties.

Security monitoring protects everyone. We watch for fraud, abuse, and threats. If we detect suspicious activity, we may restrict access or contact you.

## How We Share Information

We share information only when necessary and never sell your personal data.

**Service providers.** We work with trusted vendors for hosting, analytics, payment processing, email delivery, and customer support. These providers access data only to perform services on our behalf.

**Legal obligations.** Information may be disclosed if required by law, such as in response to a valid court order or subpoena. We object to overbroad requests when we can.

**Business transfers.** If AWFixer is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will receive notice before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

**With your consent.** Information may also be shared in other ways if you ask us to.

## Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to run our services and understand how they are used.

**Essential cookies** are required for basic functionality. They let you stay logged in and navigate the app. You cannot disable these.

**Functional cookies** remember your preferences, such as language or display settings. These make your experience smoother.

**Analytics cookies** help us understand how visitors use our site. We use this data to improve navigation and fix broken flows.

You can control non-essential cookies through your browser settings. Blocking analytics cookies will not break any features.

## Data Retention and Security

We keep your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove most data within 30 days. Some information, like billing records, may be kept longer for legal or accounting reasons.

We protect data with industry-standard practices. Data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Our infrastructure runs on cloud providers with robust physical and network security. Access to production systems is limited to employees who need it.

No system is perfect. If we discover a security breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you promptly.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data.

**Access and correction.** You can view and edit most of your account information from your profile settings.

**Deletion.** You can delete your account at any time. This removes your profile and most associated data within 30 days.

**Export.** You can export your data in standard formats where technically feasible.

**Marketing opt-out.** You can unsubscribe from promotional emails by clicking the link at the bottom of any message.

**GDPR and CCPA rights.** If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability. California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect and to request deletion. To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@awfixer.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## Third-Party Services

Our services may contain links to third-party websites or integrations with external tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Reading their privacy policies before sharing data is a good practice.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us at privacy@awfixer.com and we will delete it.

In the European Economic Area, the minimum age is 16, or the age at which your country allows you to consent to data processing.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or through an in-app notice at least 30 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of our services after that date means you accept the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, email us at **privacy@awfixer.com**. We read every message and respond within five business days.
