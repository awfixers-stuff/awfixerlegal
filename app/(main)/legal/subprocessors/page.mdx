# Subprocessors

To support delivery of our Services, AWFixer may engage data processors with access to certain Customer Data. Each subprocessor is subject to contractual terms that require protection of Customer Data consistent with the obligations in our Data Processing Agreement.

## What is a subprocessor?

A subprocessor is a third-party data processor engaged by AWFixer that processes Customer Data on our behalf. We maintain a list of all subprocessors that may handle Customer Data and review this list regularly.

## Vetting process

Before we engage any subprocessor, we conduct a security and privacy review that evaluates the vendor's technical safeguards, organizational controls, and compliance posture. We only engage subprocessors that can meet or exceed our data protection standards.

## Updates to this list

We will notify customers of new subprocessors at least 15 days before they process Customer Data. To subscribe to subprocessor updates, email [legal@awfixer.com](mailto:legal@awfixer.com).

## Objection process

Customers may object to a new subprocessor within 10 days of notification by contacting [legal@awfixer.com](mailto:legal@awfixer.com). We will work with you in good faith to address concerns. If we are unable to resolve the objection, you may terminate the applicable Service for convenience in accordance with your agreement.
